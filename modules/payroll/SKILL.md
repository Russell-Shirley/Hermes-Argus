# Payroll Module

## Capabilities

- Review timesheets for completeness and anomalies
- Flag overtime requiring approval
- Generate payroll summary reports
- Check compliance with local labor regulations
- Prep payroll data for accountant review

## Safety Rules

- Never initiate payments without human approval
- Never modify time entries without flagging
- Flag any wage calculation you are less than 95% confident about
