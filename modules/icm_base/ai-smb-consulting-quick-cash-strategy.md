# AI SMB Consulting — Quick Cash Strategy

## Overview
Research on what services an AI SMB consulting company can offer to generate quick revenue, evaluated by speed-to-cash and margin.

---

## Tier 1: Fastest / Highest Margin

### 1. AI-Powered Customer Service Chatbots
- Pre-built chatbot deployments for SMB websites
- Pricing: $500–$3,000/month
- Timeline: 1–2 weeks setup
- Notes: High demand, low delivery cost once templated

### 2. AI Content & Copywriting Services
- Using ChatGPT/Claude to produce blogs, email sequences, ad copy, social media
- Pricing: $1,000–$5,000/month retainer
- Notes: Zero infrastructure, pure service margin

### 3. AI Workflow Automation
- Automate repetitive tasks (invoicing, email responses, CRM updates, data entry)
- Tools: Zapier + AI
- Pricing: $2,000–$10,000 one-time setup per client
- Notes: Huge pain point for SMBs

---

## Tier 2: Strong Revenue, Slightly Longer Sales Cycle

### 4. AI Data Analysis & Reporting
- Connect SMB data sources (QuickBooks, Shopify, Google Analytics) to AI dashboards
- Pricing: $1,000–$4,000/month retainer

### 5. AI Sales & Lead Generation
- AI-driven outreach sequences, lead scoring, CRM enrichment
- Pricing: $2,000–$8,000 setup + monthly
- Notes: SMBs love anything that brings revenue

### 6. AI Training & Workshops
- Teach SMB teams how to use ChatGPT, Claude, Midjourney
- Format: Half-day workshops
- Pricing: $2,000–$5,000 per workshop
- Notes: Easy to sell, zero delivery risk

---

## Tier 3: Niche / Specialized

### 7. AI-Powered SEO Optimization
- AI to generate SEO-optimized content, meta tags, keyword research
- Pricing: $1,000–$3,000/month

### 8. Custom GPT / AI Assistant Builds
- Build custom GPTs for specific SMB workflows
- Pricing: $3,000–$15,000 per build

---

## Recommended Approach for Quick Cash

**Start with Tier 1**, specifically **Chatbots + Content Services**:
- Shortest sales cycle (SMBs understand the value immediately)
- Lowest technical barrier to entry
- Recurring revenue models
- Easy to scale with templates

### Pricing Strategy
Offer a "starter package" at **$997–$1,997/month** that bundles:
- Chatbot deployment
- Content production
- One workflow automation

Upsell from there.

### Sales Tactic
Target SMBs already spending on Google/Facebook ads — they have budget and understand ROI. Pitch AI as a way to reduce their ad spend through better customer engagement.
